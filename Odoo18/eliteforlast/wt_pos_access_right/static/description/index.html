Overview

This module enhances the Point of Sale (POS) system by providing administrators with the ability to restrict specific features and actions for employees. It ensures better control and security of POS operations by allowing restrictions on the following:

  • Control Buttons:
    • Quantity modification
    • Discounts
    • Price changes
    • Removing order lines
    • Increment/Decrement (+/-) operations
  • Action Buttons:
    • Canceling orders
    • Performing cash in/out operations
    • Processing refunds

With these restrictions, the module ensures that employees have limited access to sensitive functions, providing better control over POS operations.

Features

Restrict quantity modification for employees.

Restrict discount application in POS.

Restrict price changes by employees.

Restrict removing order lines in POS.

Restrict increment/decrement (+/-) operations in POS.

Restrict canceling orders by employees.

Restrict cash in/out operations in POS.

Restrict processing refunds by employees.

Configuration

  • Enable 'Log in with Employees' in POS configuration to make all employee restriction fields visible in the settings.
  • After selecting 'Log in with Employees' in the Point of Sale, the restriction fields will appear in the POS settings, enabling you to assign and manage access permissions for employees.
  • Once an employee is selected in the Basic Rights section, that employee will become visible across all restriction fields, allowing for tailored access control.
  • When an employee is selected in the restriction fields, they will not have access to the corresponding control and action buttons. The following restrictions apply:
  • Restrict Quantity: Selected employee(s) cannot modify quantities.
  • Restrict Discount: Selected employee(s) cannot apply discounts.
  • Restrict Price: Selected employee(s) cannot change product prices.
  • Restrict Remove Line: Selected employee(s) cannot remove order lines.
  • Restrict +/-: Selected employee(s) cannot perform increment or decrement actions.
  • Additionally, for action buttons:
  • Restrict Cancel Order: Selected employee(s) cannot cancel orders.
  • Restrict Cash In-Out: Selected employee(s) cannot perform cash in or cash out operations.
  • Restrict Refund Order: Selected employee(s) cannot process refund orders.
  • Employees selected in the 'Restrict Quantity', 'Restrict Discount', 'Restrict Price', 'Restrict Remove Line', and 'Restrict +/-' fields will not have access to modify quantities, apply discounts, change product prices, remove order lines, or perform increment/decrement actions.
  • Selected employees in the 'Restrict Cancel Order' field will not have access to process Cancel Order.
  • Selected employees in the 'Restrict Cash In-Out' field will not have access to process Cash In-Out.
  • Selected employees in the 'Restrict Refund Order' field will not have access to process Refund Orders.
